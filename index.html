About Us

Our NGO is committed to protecting and revitalizing Varaldevi Lake, ensuring it remains a clean and beautiful resource for our community. We organize various events, including Yagyas and Gayatri activities, to promote spiritual well-being and environmental consciousness.

Our Mission

We aim to create awareness about the importance of a clean environment, specifically focusing on Varaldevi Lake. Through our efforts, we strive to foster a sense of responsibility towards nature and inspire the community to participate in maintaining and preserving this vital water source.
